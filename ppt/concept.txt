
Pipe profiles are mentioned PR-01, PR-02… (PR=flange to flange), Most of PR are used in multiple configs i.e, 108m,119m and 18m. It is mandatory to match support location and support type.

In addition, a detailed interchangeability study has been performed across multiple configurations (18 m / 108 m / 199 m models). The resulting support locations and support types have been incorporated in the attached markup.

It should be noted that, considering the requirement for removable supports, the routing and support arrangement have been optimized to ensure that sleepers carrying Rest (R) and Rest + Guide (R+G) experience minimal lateral loads. 
Line Stop (LS) has been provided at the T-post to ensure sleeper interchangeability and to achieve optimized load distribution.
